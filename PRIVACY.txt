Information We Collect

Chiaki-Up is committed to the privacy and security of our users. We only collect and process the minimal amount of data required to provide the remote play functionality. When you use Chiaki-Up, we will collect the following information:

PSN ID: 

To enable remote play, you must provide your PlayStation Network (PSN) ID. 

This ID is used solely for the purpose of connecting to your PlayStation console for remote play and is not stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected by Chiaki-Up is used exclusively for the purpose of providing remote play functionality. We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to third parties, except as required by law or as detailed in this Privacy Policy.

Remote Play Service: 

Your PSN ID is used solely to establish a connection between the Chiaki-Up app and your PlayStation 4/5 console. It is not used for any other purpose and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Data Retention

We store your PSN ID locally for use with the Chiaki-Up app. We do not store or retain any other personal information about you. If you uninstall the app or cease using Chiaki-Up, your PSN ID will also get wiped.

Third-Party Services

Chiaki-Up is an open-source app and does not use any third-party services that collect additional personal information. Our commitment to data privacy remains confined to the essential functionality of the remote play service.
